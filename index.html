What is CourtRank?

CourtRank gives pickup sports players something they've never had — a real skill rating. After every game, your ELO updates based on who you beat and who beat you. Challenge players in your area to 1v1s, 2v2s, 3v3s, or full 5v5s. Compete on local and global leaderboards. No more arguing about who's the best at the park — let the rating speak for itself.
